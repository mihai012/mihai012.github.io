Integrations & Compatibility

Garden Reads can import highlights, notes, and reading progress from backup files you export yourself from other reading apps (such as Moon+ Reader), and can connect to your own calibre and Audiobookshelf servers to access your libraries. Imported files and server data are processed on your device.
